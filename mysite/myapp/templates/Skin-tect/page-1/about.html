About Us

We are a group of Georgia Tech Students who created this project during a hackathon. We aim to provide tools and education about how artificial intelligence (AI) can be used to detect skin cancer, while raising awareness about current bias and issues with existing skin lesion detection AI, and working to offer healthcare providers with easy tools to reduce bias in the data and improve accuracy of predictions.
