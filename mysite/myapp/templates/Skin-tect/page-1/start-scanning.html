How to Take Your Photo

Turn on Flash

Make sure that your camera’s flash is turned on for the best possible image.

Focus on Lesion

Ensure that the camera lens is focused on the area of concern and that the image is not blurry.

Crop your Image

Crop the image so that the lesion takes up one-third of the width and one-third of the height, and is centered in the photo.
