MORE INFORMATION

There are many other types of skin lesions that are not cause for concern, such as birthmarks and certain moles. All skin lesions should ideally be monitored with your with your dermatologist for changes and new symptoms because early detection of skin cancer is the easiest way to improve the likelihood of favorable outcomes.

Melanoma is the most lethal form of skin cancer, but with regular dermatologist exams, and going to see your healthcare provider if you have a concern, it can be caught early.

Basal cell carcinoma is the most common form of skin cancer, and can usually be successfully treated. See your healthcare provider as soon as possible if you suspect you have a Basal cell carcinoma.
